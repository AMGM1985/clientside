Penthouse - INDICATIVE RATE NZD $599 PER NIGHT

Our one-bedroom suites are a well-deserved escape from the lights and action of the large cities. Located on the 88th to the 90th floors high above the city rush, our suites have living rooms with stylish leather couches, club chairs and remote control flat screen TVs.

Each room is ready for business with a spacious desk featuring internet access, reading lamp and speaker phone with dual line direct dial phones with message alert and voicemail in 4 languages.

The bedrooms have all of the amenities and luxury of our Superior rooms including new stylish platform beds with pillow top mattresses, leather club chairs and remote control flat-screen TVs.

Other in-room features include floor to ceiling, functioning windows and in-room safes. Coffee/Tea makers and robes are standard in all suites.
