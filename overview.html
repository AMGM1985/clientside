Hotel Overview

Savoy Hotel Chicago is located in the heart of city, soaring at 90 stories high above the loop and North Michigan Avenue. It is a showcase of bold style, upscale ambiance and engaging design with iridescent-tinted glass. With a great location, it is a short walking distance to the best of downtown Chicago from the exclusive shops of the Magnificent Mile to the historic sites along the Riverwalk. The Savoy Chicago brings sophistication and elegance to the heart of Chicagoland.

Revel in the skyline from one of our spacious guest rooms and suites, luxuriously appointed with custom bedding and advanced technology. Experience our distinctive service, fitness center, business center and concierge staff ready to help you with the hottest tickets, directions and other arrangements.

The hotel's elegant Restaurant Charlotte serves contemporary European cuisine all day long and is ideal for pre or post show dining.

Savoy Hotels offer newly renovated meeting spaces and are the only hotels in the world with on-site meeting facilities approved by the International Association of Conference Centers (IACC). Centrally located near midtown business, our versatile and exquisite function spaces span several floors.
